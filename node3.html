Prospective Pretreatment Planning For LITT


Slicer Demo

Polynomial Chaos for Planning

A Slicer module is under development for treatment planning of MRgLITT procedures. The shown module is an extension of the Slicer ProstateNav Module. Slicer provides the infrastructure for semi-automatic segmentation of the target lesion. The "LITTPlan" module allows the surgeon to mouse the computer model predicted thermal dose onto the treatment planning images. The interactive environment allows the surgeon to optimally place the trajectory of the catheter to kill the target lesion and minimize any damage to surrounding critical structures.
