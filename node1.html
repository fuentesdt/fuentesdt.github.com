Planning and Simulation Software for LITT. FEM predictions of the bioheat transfer are used for treatment planning of a hippocampus ablation to treat epilepsy. A volume rendering of the pre-treatment planning is shown. Critical structures and boundaries are segmented and used to generate a volumetric hexahedral mesh. Multiple laser applicators are used to deliver the desired thermal dose. A line profile (temperature vs distance ) is shown at the bottom. The ventricles if modeled as a heat sink for the bioheat transfer.
